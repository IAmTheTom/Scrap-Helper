# Refrigerant Equipment Exclusions

- **Hazard category:** Refrigerant and sealed-system handling
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Applies to:** Refrigerators, freezers, air conditioners, dehumidifiers, heat pumps, and similar sealed systems
- **Status:** Locked exclusion

## Policy

Scrap Helper's normal beginner collection workflow excludes equipment containing sealed refrigerant systems unless the user has confirmed lawful, safe, and yard-accepted handling by qualified personnel.

## Excluded Actions

- cutting refrigerant lines;
- venting refrigerant;
- removing compressors from charged systems;
- puncturing evaporators or condensers;
- assuming an abandoned appliance is empty;
- accepting equipment that the local yard refuses.

## Recognition

Items likely to contain refrigerant include:

- refrigerators;
- freezers;
- window air conditioners;
- portable air conditioners;
- central-air components;
- heat pumps;
- many dehumidifiers;
- refrigerated vending or display equipment.

## Safe Handling

- Leave the sealed system intact.
- Confirm yard acceptance before pickup.
- Follow local disposal rules.
- Use qualified refrigerant recovery when required.
- Do not treat the compressor as ordinary removable scrap until the system is properly handled.

## App Behavior

These items should display:

- exclusion warning;
- yard-confirmation requirement;
- no dismantling recommendation;
- no default recovered-compressor estimate.
