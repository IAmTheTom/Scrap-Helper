# Yard Questions Checklist

Use this checklist before assuming how a yard will grade or accept material.

## General

- What identification or account information is required?
- What are current hours?
- Are there minimum weights?
- Are prices posted publicly?
- Are ferrous and nonferrous materials weighed separately?
- Are there ticket, processing, or disposal fees?

## Category Questions

- What is your current name for light mixed steel?
- Do filing cabinets need drawers removed?
- Are stainless grill panels bought as stainless if magnetic hardware remains?
- Are transformers bought separately?
- Are electric motors bought separately?
- Are power cords separate from insulated copper wire?
- Are low-grade circuit boards accepted?
- Are aluminum wheels required to be bare?
- Must valve stems and wheel weights be removed?
- Are cast-iron grill grates separate from shred steel?

## Appliance Questions

- Are washers, dryers, dishwashers, ranges, and microwaves accepted whole?
- Are any appliance types charged a fee?
- Are refrigerant appliances accepted?
- What proof of refrigerant removal is required?
- Are microwave transformers accepted separately?

## Grill Questions

- Must propane tanks be removed?
- Must regulators, hoses, grease trays, or plastic shelves be removed?
- How is stainless verified?
- Are cast-iron grates separate?

## Receipt Questions

- Does the receipt show:
  - category;
  - weight;
  - unit;
  - rate;
  - line payout;
  - deductions?
