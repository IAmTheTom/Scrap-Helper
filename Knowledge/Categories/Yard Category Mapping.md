# Yard Category Mapping

## Purpose

Map each yard's exact printed or spoken category name to a canonical Scrap Helper category.

## Rules

- Preserve the yard's wording exactly.
- Record the effective date.
- Keep historical mappings when the yard changes terminology.
- Do not assume another yard uses the same grading standard.
- Note preparation requirements and known exclusions.
