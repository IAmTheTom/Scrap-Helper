# Claim Status Definitions

## Draft
The claim has been recorded but not reviewed.

## Unverified
The claim lacks sufficient evidence or relies on one weak source.

## Partially Supported
Some evidence exists, but the claim remains model-dependent, incomplete, or conflicting.

## Supported
The claim is backed by reliable evidence appropriate to its type.

## Measured
The value was directly measured and the measurement record is preserved.

## Receipt Confirmed
The category, rate, weight, or payout was confirmed by an actual scrapyard receipt.

## Yard Confirmed
The rule or category was confirmed directly by the scrapyard through a website, price sheet, phone call, or in-person communication.

## Disputed
Reliable sources conflict and no resolution has been established.

## Rejected
The claim was found inaccurate, misleading, unsafe, or irrelevant.

## Superseded
A newer or better-supported claim replaces it, while history is retained.
