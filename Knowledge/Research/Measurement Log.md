## Required Measurement Details

Each detailed record should include:

- scale used;
- scale resolution;
- tare method;
- object condition;
- components removed;
- recovery level;
- material categories;
- individual and total weights;
- processing time;
- photos when useful;
- related load and receipt IDs.
